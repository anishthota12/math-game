Info Box

This was created to help people with math. I hope you enjoy the games and learn math from here!

Normal: Game. You have 120 seconds to answer as many math problems as you can. You can adjust the settings using the buttons at the top.
Normal: Practice. Answer math problems with unlimited time. Adjust the operation and number ranges with the buttons at the top.
Bubble: Game. The sum number is shown at the top. Click on the bubbles that equals the sum number. You have three lives to click as many correct bubbles as possible!
